### Page 6 — FOUNDER Content in Practice
- Headline: From Idea to Post: The FOUNDER Content Workflow
- Subhead: A repeatable system for turning your story into consistent content
- Body: The biggest barrier to FOUNDER content isn't having a story — everyone has one. The barrier is not having a system to turn that story into consistent, strategic content. Here's the workflow we use with every GRM partner.
- Step 1: MINE — Spend 20 minutes writing answers to three questions: Why did I start? What do I believe? What have I learned? These become your content bank.
- Step 2: FORMAT — Each answer becomes a short-form video (60-90 seconds), a caption, and a pull quote. One story, three formats.
- Step 3: SCHEDULE — FOUNDER content goes out once per week, every week. Not when you feel inspired. Every week.
- Step 4: CONNECT — End every FOUNDER post with a soft CTA: "If this resonates, I'd love to connect." Not a hard sell. A door open.

- Callout: Consistency is the strategy. The agent who shows up every week for 52 weeks owns the market. Most agents quit at week 6.
