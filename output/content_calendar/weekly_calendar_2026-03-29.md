## NEWOWNER — Friday

**Topic:** Five simple home maintenance tasks every new Marion County homeowner should knock out before Florida's rainy season kicks in.

**Hook:** If you just bought your first home in Ocala, this Florida rainy season will humble you fast — unless you do these five things first.

**Caption:**
Marion County's rainy season (hello, June through September) brings daily afternoon storms, humidity that means business, and the kind of standing water that loves to find every gap in your gutters and window seals — so getting ahead of it now is one of the smartest things a new homeowner can do. Start with these five: clean and extend your gutters, check your roof for loose shingles, test your sump pump if you have one, seal any exterior gaps around doors and windows, and make sure your yard has proper drainage away from your foundation. The latest issue of *The Front Porch* magazine is packed with local tips like these, written specifically for new homeowners putting down roots right here in Marion County.
