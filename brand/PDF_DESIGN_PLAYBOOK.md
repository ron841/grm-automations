# GRM PDF Design Playbook
**For Manus — read this before building any GRM lead magnet PDF.**
Last updated: March 22, 2026
---
## 1. DOCUMENT SPECS
- Page size: 8.5 x 11 inches (612 x 792 PDF points)
- Margins: 0.6 inch left/right, standard top/bottom
- Color palette:
  - Teal (primary): #00B7CE
  - Dark (backgrounds, text): #090C0B
  - White: #FFFFFF
  - Cream (light backgrounds): #FAF8F4
  - Dark gray (body text): #444444
  - Light gray (dividers): #CCCCCC
- Fonts: Bold headlines (Montserrat or similar sans-serif), Italic subheads (Georgia or similar serif), Body (clean sans-serif)
- Header bar: Teal (#00B7CE), full width, 0.28 inch tall, white bold caps label centered
- Footer: GRM logo icon + "Get Rooted Media" left, publication names + page number right. Teal rule line above footer.

## 2. PHOTO RULES (CRITICAL)
- Every photo must be unique — no photo used more than once within a document, and no photo shared between FOUNDER and LISTINGS or any two pillar PDFs
- Photo rendering: ALWAYS use fill-and-crop (scale to fill frame, clip overflow) — NEVER stretch to fit
- Minimum resolution: 800px wide for full-bleed, 600px wide for strips
- Overlay: Only add dark overlay when quote text must be white over a photo background. If text is dark/charcoal on a light background, no overlay needed.
- Overlay alpha when used: 0.55 to 0.72 maximum — never darker
### Florida Visual Identity Rules
- Flat terrain only — no hills, mountains, or rocky landscapes anywhere in frame
- Must show at least one of: Spanish moss, palm trees, live oak canopy, lush green grass, crystal springs water, or Florida residential architecture
- Acceptable subjects:
  - Silver Springs / Rainbow Springs crystal water
  - Downtown Ocala (Marion Theatre area, Ocala Square)
  - Live oak canopy roads
  - Horse farms (flat pasture, white fences, grazing horses)
  - Florida ranch or Mediterranean homes with lush green lawns
  - Neighborhood streets with palm trees
  - Marion County aerial estate homes
  - Florida parks and playground areas
  - Florida sunset / golden hour landscapes (flat terrain only)
- NOT acceptable: mountains, generic city skylines, Pacific Northwest trees, UK or European streets, MacBook or coffee shop scenes, brick wall backgrounds, stock watermarks of any kind (Getty, Alamy, Shutterstock, Adobe)
### Horse Photo Limit
Maximum 2-3 clearly visible horse photos across BOTH documents combined. Ocala is more than horses — prioritize Silver Springs, downtown Ocala, neighborhoods, live oak roads, and community scenes.
### Photo Sourcing
Use Pexels search API by keyword. Always verify the photo visually before placing. Reject anything that does not feel like Marion County, Florida. Search terms that work well: "Ocala Florida", "Silver Springs Florida", "live oak canopy road Florida", "horse farm white fence Florida", "Florida neighborhood palm trees", "Spanish moss Florida".

## 3. BADGE + HEADLINE SPACING
- Pill badges (FOUNDER, PLAYBOOK, NEWOWNER, LISTINGS, LOCAL, SOCIAL PROOF) must have minimum 8pt clear space between badge bottom and headline top
- Badge draws from y - bh to y; headline draws at y - bh - gap where gap >= 22pt
- Never let a badge overlap or crowd a headline
- Badge background colors: #00B7CE (teal) for active pillar, #090C0B (dark) for supporting pillars
---
## 4. PULL QUOTE PAGES
- Option A: Full-bleed photo + dark overlay (0.55 alpha) + white centered quote text
- Option B: Full-bleed photo at full brightness + dark/charcoal quote text with teal left accent line
- Never use editorial spreads or competitor brand imagery as photo backgrounds
- Quote must appear in the lower third of the image when possible
- Never let the same quote appear twice on the same page — if there is a teal callout box AND a photo overlay quote, remove one. One quote treatment per page only.
---
## 5. PUBLICATION PAGES (Closing Table, Front Porch)
- These pages feature GRM's own publications
- Hero photo must match the editorial tone of the publication:
  - The Closing Table: Spanish moss, live oak, professional real estate setting, warm rural Florida
  - The Front Porch: welcoming home exterior, Florida neighborhood, family arriving, community feel
- Bottom photo strip and hero photo must feel cohesive — same warm Florida energy, not two different vibes
- The Closing Table brand color: #00B7CE teal
- The Front Porch brand color: #00B7CE teal (same GRM system)
- Never use a photo on a publication page that appears anywhere else in the same document
---
## 6. CARD AND TABLE RULES
- Approved card background colors: #090C0B (dark) and #00B7CE (teal) only
- Alternate between dark and teal on sequential cards
- Never use purple, violet, blue-gray, forest green, gold, or any color not in the approved palette
- All text inside cards must be fully visible — no truncation with ellipsis (...)
- If text does not fit, reduce font size to minimum 10pt or tighten copy — never cut the sentence
- Zoom to 150% on every card before approving final render
---
## 7. ATTRIBUTION FORMAT
- Pull quote attributions: "— First Last, Title, Location"
- Example: "— Cameron Cowart, Top 20 Agent, Marion County"
- Example: "— Get Rooted Media, Marion County"
- Never attribute a client-voice quote to "Get Rooted Media" — use real or representative names only

## 10. THREE-BRAND ARCHITECTURE (Never violate this)
GET ROOTED MEDIA is always the parent publisher — appears on every page in footer.
THE CLOSING TABLE is the agent-facing publication.
THE FRONT PORCH is the homeowner-facing publication.
- GRM logo appears on cover and footer of every PDF
- Never lead with a sub-brand without GRM above it
- All five pillar PDFs belong to the GRM brand system — not to CT or FP individually
